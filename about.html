🏠 Home

Welcome to Your Pocket Guide to Japan 🇯🇵

Learn Japanese for Travel is your go-to, no-pressure, super-friendly way to learn the language and customs of Japan—especially if you're planning to visit soon (or dreaming about it!).

🌟 What's Inside?

  • Essential Phrases: From “Konnichiwa” to “Help!”, we’ve got you covered with real-life Japanese phrases + audio.
  • Quick Flashcards: Practice on the plane, train, or while sipping matcha at home.
  • Interactive Quizzes: Test what you’ve learned (or just challenge a friend).
  • Mini Tutorials: Learn to say things the right way—and why that matters.
  • Cultural Etiquette: No more guessing—know how to bow, bathe, and be polite.
  • Numbers, Time, Directions: Understand signs, schedules, and how to get around smoothly.

📱 Made for Real Travelers

We made this site lightweight and mobile-friendly—so you can pull it up anywhere in Japan, even with spotty Wi-Fi. No logins. No accounts. Just free, helpful Japanese.

💡 Why We Made This

Because knowing even a little Japanese can unlock a lot of experiences: friendlier smiles, better meals, smoother travel, and a deeper connection with an incredible culture.

And because we love Japan. And we know you will too.
