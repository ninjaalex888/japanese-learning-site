Pronunciation Tips

Japanese vowels are pronounced: A (ah), I (ee), U (oo), E (eh), O (oh). Each syllable has equal length.

Politeness Levels

Use polite forms like "です" (desu) and "ます" (masu) with strangers and elders. Casual forms are used among close friends.

Cultural Tips

  • Bowing is a common greeting and sign of respect.
  • Remove shoes before entering a home.
  • It's polite to say "いただきます" before meals and "ごちそうさま" after eating.
  • Use both hands when giving or receiving something important.
